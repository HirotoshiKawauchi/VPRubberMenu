VPRubberMenu

UICollectionView - Menu component with 'rubbery' effect inspired by Nike 'Making' application. Created just for fun :)

Usage

Use VPRubberLayout with your UICollectionView as UICollectionViewFlowLayout. Use VPRubberCell as your UICollectionViewCell. Use VPRubberSettings for configuring animation.
